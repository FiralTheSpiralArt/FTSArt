Hi! My name is FiralTheSpiral, or at least, that's what I go by online. Anyways, I'm a North American cartoon artist in the middle of high school. I specialize in simple cartoon drawings, but I can do realism depending on what it is. I use either hatching or cross hatching for my shading tecnique. I use a "Huion H640P" drawing tablet for all of my digital art. If you want something commissioned, your best bet would be to message me on Instagram @firalthespiral, but I am available through email, at firalthespiralart@gmail.com, if you dont have Instagram, though, i probably wont respond as quickly. I currently only accept payment through paypal, but that is subject to change. Anyways, I hope you like my art!
